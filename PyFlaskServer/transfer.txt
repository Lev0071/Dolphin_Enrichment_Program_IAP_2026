Zip on Windows ( Windows PowerShell):
Compress-Archive -Path PyFlaskServer          
   -DestinationPath PyFlaskServer_clean.zip    
   -CompressionLevel Optimal                   
   -Exclude "__pycache__", "*.pyc", "*.pyo",   
            "venv", ".vscode", ".idea"         

Zip Raspberry Pi (Linux):
 zip -r PyFlaskServer_clean.zip PyFlaskServer  
   -x "PyFlaskServer/venv/*"                   
   -x "PyFlaskServer/__pycache__/*"           
   -x "*.pyc" "*.pyo"                          
   -x "*/.vscode/*" "*/.idea/*"
